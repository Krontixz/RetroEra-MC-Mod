# Retro-Edition-MINECRAFT-MOD

## THIS IS A MINECRAFT MOD

### A MINECRAFT MOD TO GIVE THAT RETRO FEEL

#### WITHOUT BREAKING YOUR EARS!

## HOW TO USE:

**To use this mod, you need to learn some commands to change the sound sample rate,**
**they are listed below**

To set the sample rate to 8000hz (or Khz) type in this command:

/function retro_edition:set_rate {samplerate:"8000"}

To set the sample rate to 11025hz (or Khz) type in this command:

/function retro_edition:set_rate {samplerate:"11025"}

### THE SOUNDS THIS MOD EDITS/REPLACES:

**1. The sounds/ambient/cave/ sounds, they are the sounds that play when you enter caves randomly.**
**2. The sounds/ambient/weather/thunder.ogg sounds, they are the sounds that play when lightning strikes.**
**3. The sounds/random/explode.ogg sounds, they are the sounds that play when something explodes, like a creeper, or TnT.**
**4. The sounds/mob/ghast/ sounds, they are the sounds that the ghast makes when it sees you. Or after it ate tacobell...**
